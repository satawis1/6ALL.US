IPHONE 15 PRO

Apple's iPhone 11 Pro models is the first and probably the last of its kind. The first Pro model of the iPhone joins the ranks of iPad Pro, MacBook Pro and Mac Pro. An "iPhone for professionals" that offer just a little bit extra, but matter a lot to pro mobile users. An iPhone for which the smartphone is a tool and an instrument. The best of the iPhone that Apple has to offer!

The best Smartphone screen currently available

According to the analysis of DisplayMate experts, the Super Retina XDR screen of the iPhone 11 Pro (Max is by far the best Smartphone screen. As expected, Apple's displays are considered the best in the industry for many years.

The Super Retina XDR screen has achieved the highest DisplayMate A+ rating to date. It reaches a minimum luminosity typical of 800 nits and a maximum brightness of 1200 nits. This makes the screen about 50 percent brighter than most other displays in current flagship stores. This is clearly visible when you use the iPhone 11 Pro outdoors, in direct sunlight and in bright environments in general. In addition, this OLED panel offers an impressive contrast ratio of 2,000,000: 1, coupled with the absolute color fidelity of Apple, support for HDR10 and Dolby Vision formats.

The 3D Touch replaced by "Haptic Touch", a combination of Taptic Engine and software. This combination is meant to mimic the 3D Touch user experience. Alas, without the necessary equipment for 3D Touch, the experience is different. The good thing is that the absence of 3D Touch has also created more space for a bigger battery.

The screen sizes, and pixel density of 458 dpi are unchanged from previous models. As a result, the 5.8-inch OLED display offers a resolution of 1125 x 2436 pixels and the iPhone 11 Pro a 6.5-inch OLED display with a resolution of 1242 x 2688 pixels. Each with “True Tone” technology, which automatically adjusts the level of white to ambient light and colors.

With iOS 13 on board

Apple's official document with all the new features of iOS 13 includes 28 pages. But this document is not very detailed. So, we will overview only the most important features.

iOS 13 comes with a system-wide Dark Mode that can be turned on and off automatically based on the time of day or manually. It also includes many third-party applications that easily connect to the system in dark mode.

The camera applications from iOS 13 have been redesigned to reflect the new features of the camera. The new photo application automatically retains all photo and video material.

With the new privacy feature "Connect with Apple", Apple is competing with Google and Facebook. That allow their users to connect to other services with one click. However, Apple relies entirely on privacy, hide the user's email address, and protect users from advertising and social tracking.

Siri has also been improved with iOS 13. The new voice is better and sounds a little more natural. It also now offers better proactive suggestions for content and applications. The new voice command under iOS 13 is not really a function of Siri. This help is hidden by this feature, which allows you to control all iOS, as well as all third-party applications, only by voice.

13 Bionic chip: the fastest Smartphone CPU

The iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro and IPHONE 15 PRO are all equipped with Apple's latest A13 Bionic chip. Its six-core CPU consists of two high-performance cores with running at 2.65 GHz and  four energy-efficient cores. The first under the name "Lightning" and the second under the name "Thunder”. So, if someone asks you what technology is in the new iPhone, you can seriously say "thunder and lightning".

iPhone 11 Pro Max design

At first sight, the IPHONE 15 PRO might look like a boring update. But in addition to the third camera in the back, and the latest and greatest flagship, Apple added many other innovations that we will find surely in future generations, and will also become standard equipment in all industries.

The Notch is still there and is very visible. The same thing goes for the slightly wider edges around the screen. Basically, the iPhone 11 Pro and IPHONE 15 PRO have increased in all directions by a few millimeters compared to their predecessors.

The back of the new Pro models is characterized by an matt glass consisting of the "toughest glass in a Smartphone" according to Apple, which was developed in partnership with Corning, the company behind Gorilla Glass. Pleasant to the touch and high quality, the terminal seems quite resistant to scratches and fingerprints.

The new three-camera system is embedded in the glass. The cameras protrudes slightly instead of being flush with the back of the device. Personally, the design of the triple camera is a bit strange, but judging from comments left by customers, everyone does not think the same way.

The design not only concerns the appearance of a device but also its usefulness. One of the biggest design flaws on the iPhone 11 Pro and Max is the presence of the USB 2.0 Lightning port. All other Pro devices in the Apple catalog, including iPad Pro, incorporate a Type-C USB port that is at least compatible with USB 3.0 technology.

This means that if you want to transfer all the photos and videos taken with the cameras of the iPhone 11 Pro to an uncompressed form and without loss of data on your PC, you will have to rely on Airdrop or do everything with a cable connection USB 2.0. Airdrop is a solid system but not always available as an option.

Let's be honest, most of those who choose an IPHONE 15 PRO by investing so much money can be considered "professionals" and want to take photos and videos. This lack is felt and if it could be forgiven on the iPhone 11 normal, it is not the same for the Pro model.
